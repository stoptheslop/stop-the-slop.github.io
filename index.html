Stop The Slop

A manifesto for genuine design and engineering.

The Backdrop

In an era of rapid digital production, we’ve seen a surge in what we call “slop”—low-quality, derivative, and disposable content and software that prioritizes quantity over craftsmanship.

This manifesto was born out of a desire to champion the human element in creation and engineering. We believe that while technology like AI is a powerful enhancer, it should never be a replacement for thoughtful design, robust architecture, and genuine human ingenuity.

The Manifesto

We believe in craftsmanship over quantity.

We believe in building durable, thoughtful solutions, not disposable digital landfill.

AI is a powerful tool, but it should augment human ingenuity, not replace it with low-value, derivative 'slop'.
